=================
Structure Writer
=================

An extension of ASE's `lattice factories <https://wiki.fysik.dtu.dk/ase/ase/lattice.html#defining-new-lattices>`_ and `ClusterFactory <https://wiki.fysik.dtu.dk/ase/epydoc/ase.cluster.factory.ClusterFactory-class.html>`_.

Lattices
========

	* - diamond
	* - graphene
	* - graphite
	* - simple tetragonal (beta-Tin)
	* - SnO2-rutile phase

Clusters
========
	* - Octahedron (commandline control for modified octahedron)
	* - Wulff construction

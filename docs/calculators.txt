=========
Calculators
=========

Several calculators are included in addition to calculators which can be run through `ASE <https://wiki.fysik.dtu.dk/ase/ase/calculators/calculators.html#module-calculators>_`

**calculators**: the type of calculator to execute

        ``eam_al``: Embedded atom method parameterized for aluminum.

        ``bopfox``: Bond order potential, for metals. 

        ``ljocl``: Lennard-Jones potential in reduced unit with openCL [#ljocl]_

        ``meam_si``: Modified-EAM potential, for silicon. [#swsi]_

        ``meam_si``: Modified-EAM potential, for lithium and silicon. [#meamsi]_

	``lepsho``: LEPS+ Harmonic Oscillator potential. [#lepsho]_

	``push``: Simple potential to push apart atoms to minimal distance

	``Voter77``: Voter77 potential.  [#voter77]_
	
	``w``: Molybdenum potential. [#w]_

	``ZDP_5Gaus``: ZDP 5Gaus potential. [#ZDP5Gaus]_
